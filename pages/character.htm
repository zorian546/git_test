Character Analysis

Itadori Yuji

A kind-hearted and athletic high schooler who becomes a Jujutsu Sorcerer after swallowing a cursed object—Sukuna’s finger. He’s selfless, driven by a strong sense of justice, and struggles with the moral weight of killing curses that were once human.

Megumi Fushiguro

A stoic and intelligent student with a mysterious past. He uses the Ten Shadows Technique and believes in protecting others based on their potential to do good, rather than simple morality. He’s deeply introspective and loyal to his friends.

Nobara Kugisaki

A confident and fierce sorcerer with a strong fashion sense and stronger fists. She uses a hammer, nails, and voodoo-style techniques to fight. Nobara balances her pride in her identity with genuine care for her teammates.

Satoru Gojo

he strongest Jujutsu Sorcerer and teacher at Jujutsu High. Charismatic, laid-back, and borderline arrogant, Gojo hides a serious desire to reform the corrupt Jujutsu system and protect the next generation of sorcerers.
